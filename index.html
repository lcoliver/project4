Women's Rugby

An Emerging Sport

Women's rugby has progressed in leaps and bound over the past decade in the United States. Often compared to the growth of women's soccer, rugby is steadily gaining popularity and emerging as a potential new favorite pastime for Americans, as well as a means of empowerment for women specifically. The sport is considered one of the most egalitarian sports today, with all rules and equipment being the same for men and women. The sport encourages women to participate in an aggressive role that many women find to be liberating, though new and strange at first. Women's rugby is currently classified as an NCAA emerging sport and currently there are 13 full varsity teams in the country.
